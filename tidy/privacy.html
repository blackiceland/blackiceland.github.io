Privacy Policy — TidyCells

Effective date: 2025-08-28

Overview

TidyCells is a Google Sheets™ add-on for duplicate detection and data cleaning. We respect your privacy and comply with the Google API Services User Data Policy, including the Limited Use requirements.

Data We Process

When you use the add-on, we process spreadsheet content you select to analyze and technical metadata necessary to operate the service. We do not sell or share your data.

How We Use Google User Data

  • Read spreadsheet ranges to compute exact and fuzzy duplicates.
  • Write results back to the active spreadsheet when you confirm actions.
  • Display UI elements (sidebar/dialogs) to present results and Undo.

We do not use Google user data for advertising or to build unrelated profiles.

Storage and Retention

We do not persist spreadsheet contents on our servers. Processing is stateless and limited to fulfilling your in-app requests. Minimal diagnostic logs, if enabled, may include non-content technical metadata and are retained only as needed to maintain reliability.

Security

We apply industry-standard measures to protect data in transit and at rest where applicable.

Your Choices and Data Deletion

You can revoke the add-on’s access at any time in your Google Account permissions. To request deletion of any associated data or logs, email tidycells@gmail.com. We will complete the request within a reasonable timeframe.

Contact

Email: tidycells@gmail.com

Changes

We may update this policy from time to time. The latest version will always be available at tidycells.com/privacy.html.
